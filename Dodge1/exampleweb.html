Introduction

The Dodge Demon is not just a car; it's a phenomenon in the muscle car world. With its unbridled power and revolutionary features, it stands as the pinnacle of automotive engineering and performance.

By Shyam Sundar

World’s Fastest 0-60mph Production Car

The Dodge Demon reaches 60mph from a standstill in just 2.3 seconds. With a roll-out, this time reduces to 2.1 seconds, outpacing even the Bugatti Chiron and Tesla P100D with Ludicrous Plus.

Highest Horsepower V8 Engine

The Demon is equipped with an 840bhp and 770lb ft of torque 6.2-litre HEMI V8 engine, making it the most powerful production muscle car ever. It includes 25 major component upgrades over the Hellcat engine.

World’s Fastest Quarter-Mile Production Car

The Demon completes the quarter-mile in just 9.65 seconds at 140mph, surpassing the latest ultracars from McLaren, Porsche, and Ferrari.

Too Fast for the NHRA

The Demon is banned by the NHRA for its outrageous quarter-mile time. Any car that runs under 10 seconds or over 135mph requires an NHRA certified cage, which the Demon lacks as standard.

Record-Breaking Wheelie

The Dodge Demon is the world’s first production car to perform a wheelie at launch, lifting its front wheels 2.92 feet off the ground and earning a place in the Guinness Book of World Records.

The $1 Demon Crate

For just $1, you can purchase the Demon Crate, which includes a performance powertrain control module, high-octane engine calibration, conical performance air filter, and a range of tools and accessories to transform your Demon from road-ready to track-ready.

First Car with Drag Radials

The Demon is the first production car to be fitted with street-legal drag-racing tyres, the Nitto NT05Rs. These tyres offer a larger contact patch and more than twice the grip of the Challenger SRT Hellcat.

The Future of the Demon

In 2023, Dodge introduced the 1011hp Dodge Demon 170, the last of the seven "Last Call" editions, continuing the legacy of the Demon with even more power and performance.
